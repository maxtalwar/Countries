Local Food

Char Kway Teow is a stir-fried noodle dish. It is made from noodles, garlic, soy sauce, prawns, sausage, and bean sprouts.

Nasi Lemak is a dish consisting of rice cooked in coconut milk, fried chicken, nuts, eggs, and various vegetables.

Tourist Destinations

Gardens by the Bay is a park spanning 250 acres adjacent to the Marina Reservoir.

Merlion Park is major Singaporean Landmark located near the central business district. It was built in August 1972.

Activities

Adventure Cove Water Park is located on Sentosa, an island off the southern coast of Singapore.

Marina Bay Sands is an iconic resort, hotel, and shopping mall overlooking the Marina Bay.
